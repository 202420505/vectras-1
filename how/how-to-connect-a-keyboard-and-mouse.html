How to Connect a Keyboard and Mouse






Step 1 : Get an OTG Adapter


Go to Any Local Store and Buy an OTG Adapter (a Converter from Micro-USB or USB Type C(Check your Phone Port) to a USB Type A)

and Get a USB Hub (a Converter from 1 USB Type A to 2,3 or 4 USB Type A)


Step 2 : Connect Them to Your Phone


Connect The OTG to Your Phone and The USB Hub to Your OTG Then Connect Your Keyboard and Mouse to The USB Hub


Step 3 : Setup The Mouse to Work Inside the Rom


After You Finish Connecting Everything Go to Vectras VM and Enter The Rom You Like The Keyboard Will Work Automatically

But The Mouse Will Not so You Have to Go to on the Top Notch

Then Mouse Settings and Choose "Bluetooth/USB Mouse Option" and Then Close the Menu

Then the Mouse will Work Just Fine

If You Want to Hide the Controlles You Can Press on the Back Button on The Dpad Option and Choose Mode
